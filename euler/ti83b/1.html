Multiples of 3 or 5 (TI-83 BASIC)

I didn't want to solve this by just iterating through all the possible numbers, so I generalized the formula of summing 1 through N to allow only counting numbers that are multiples of (in this case) D. The formula is floor((end - start + 1) / divisor) / 2 * ((ceil(start / divisor) + floor(end / divisor)) * divisor. Since the TI-83 has int(A) in place of floor but does not have a ceil function, I substituted -int(-A).

1→S
999→E
3→L₁(1)
5→L₁(2)
L₁(1)*L₁(2)→L₁(3)
For(I,1,3,1)
L₁(I)→D
int((E-S+1)/D)/2*((⁻int(⁻S/D))+int(E/D))*D→L₂(I)
End
