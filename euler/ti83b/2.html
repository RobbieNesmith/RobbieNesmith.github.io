Even Fibonacci Numbers (TI-83 BASIC)

Barely optimized from a basic Fibonacci calculation. I used the fact that even numbers are spaced every third number in the sequence and rearranged terms to only need even numbers to calculate the next even number. Using fibn=4*fibn-3+fibn-6, I calculated all the required even Fibonacci numbers and summed them.

{1,2}→L₁
While dim(L₁)<5
L₁(dim(L₁)-1)+L₁(dim(L₁))→L₁(dim(L₁)+1)
End
{L₁(2),L₁(5)}→L₂
While L₂(dim(L₂))<4000000:
L₂(dim(L₂))*4+L₂(dim(L₂)-1)→L₂(dim(L₂)+1)
End
dim(L₂)-1→dim(L₂)
Disp "SUM:"
Disp sum(L₂)
